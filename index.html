Our Mission

A Focused Approach to Acquisition

Our mission is simple yet impactful: to acquire, operate, and grow a single exceptional business in the US. Our team brings a unique blend of strategic insights and operational expertise — offering business owners a flexible succession alternative that ensures their legacy lives on.

Backed by a robust network of seasoned investors and operators, we focus on creating long-term value for all stakeholders. We're actively seeking profitable businesses with owners considering a transition.

Our commitment doesn't stop at acquisition. We promise active, hands-on management and a growth-oriented approach over a 10+ year horizon — a genuine partnership, not a quick flip.

Acquisition Criteria

Essential Services

Businesses providing non-discretionary services with consistent demand regardless of economic cycles.

Recurring Revenue

Contracts, maintenance agreements, or natural repeat purchase cycles that create predictable cash flow.

EBITDA $400K–$2M

Profitable businesses in our target size range with clean financials and strong cash flow fundamentals.

04

Target Geographies

Major US metro areas including Los Angeles, Dallas, Houston, New York, Atlanta, Chicago, and more.

05

Owner Transition

Owners considering retirement or new pursuits who want a trustworthy successor to carry their legacy forward.

06

Established Operations

10+ years in business with proven processes, loyal customers, and a team capable of operating independently.
